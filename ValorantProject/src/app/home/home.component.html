What is Valorant?

Valorant is a tactical shooter played in a 5v5 format, where teams engage in intense battles. Each agent has unique abilities that can change the course of the game. The main objective is to plant or defuse the bomb, depending on the team's role, or to eliminate the opposing team. This game combines strategy, teamwork, and precision to deliver a unique competitive experience.
